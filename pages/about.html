Learn more about Gamma Pi

Our Mission

Alpha Phi Omega is a national co-educational service fraternity grounded in the principles of Leadership, Friendship, and Service. Our mission is to prepare tomorrow’s leaders through service to others. As a chapter, we take this mission to heart, striving to embody these values in everything we do—from our dynamic leadership development programs to our spirited service initiatives and social events.

A Legacy of Leadership

Since our chapter’s founding, Gamma Pi has been a hub of leadership and service at the University of Michigan for over 80 years. Our members are movers and shakers on campus and beyond. We take pride in the diverse backgrounds and experiences that each of our members brings to the table, making us a vibrant and inclusive community. Whether you’re looking to sharpen your leadership skills, contribute to meaningful service projects, or just find your home away from home, Gamma Pi has a place for you.

Friendship for Life

In Gamma Pi, friendships are formed that last a lifetime. We’re more than just a fraternity; we’re a family. Our members support each other through thick and thin, creating bonds that extend far beyond graduation. With a calendar packed full of social events, brotherhood retreats, and casual hangouts, there’s always something happening in Gamma Pi that brings us closer together.

Service with a smile

Service isn’t just something we do; it’s who we are. From local food drives and environmental clean-ups to supporting national causes, our chapter is committed to making the world a better place—one act of kindness at a time. But it’s not all work and no play! We know how to have a good time, and we often mix service with fun, whether it's through themed service events, collaborative projects with other organizations, or our famous service marathons.

Join Us

Ready to make your mark? Whether you’re a freshman looking for a place to belong, a senior aiming to leave a legacy, or somewhere in between, Gamma Pi invites you to join us on this incredible journey. Come for the service, stay for the friendship, and leave as a leader. We can’t wait to meet you!
