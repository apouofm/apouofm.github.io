Alpha Phi Omega @ the University of Michigan

Speed Friending

Tuesday, January 16th at 5:00pm in League Basement

This is a chance for you to get to know your fellow rushees and current APO active members!

Crafts for Motts

Tuesday, January 16th at 6:00pm in the League Basement

Crafts for Motts is one of our weekly recurring service opportunities where you can make crafts to donate to the children at Mott’s Children's Hospital. This event can provide you a bit of an idea of what it is like to go to a service event with your APO brothers.

Professional Development Event with Maggie Katz

Thursday, January 18th at 6:30pm in EQ1423

Maggie Katz is one of Gamma Pi’s advisors and was also the first woman to serve as the APO National President. Maggie will be talking about how you can use APO to help further develop yourself professionally, such as how to talk about it in interviews for grad school, how to market it on your resume and CV, and how to incorporate the values of APO into your daily life.

Game Night + Canned Food Drive

Saturday, January 20th at 6:00pm in EQB830.

Game night is a chance to have fun with members of APO and your fellow rushees! Please bring any board games or card games that you like! Additionally, we will be having a canned food drive, so please bring any canned food you may want to donate.

Applications Due!!

Applications are due on Saturday, January 20th at 11:59pm.

If something is barring you from being able to fill out the application before this deadline please email me and we can work something out. APO has a non-competitive rush and application process. You just need to attend half of the events and fill out the application and you are welcome to pledge with us this winter! Please answer the application sincerely so we get to know you better, but understand that the applications are not evaluated competitively, just on completion:

Initiation: After you attend the rush events and fill out the application, you are welcome to be initiated into APO during your first pledge meeting on Sunday, January 21st at 6:30pm.

More information will be sent out closer to the date about the location. The dress code is business casual - we ask that you please adhere to the dress code!
